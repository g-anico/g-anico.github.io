Nate and Damien Tribute Page


Nate and Damien are my two nephews. I love them more than life itself. It doesn't make any sense. Sometimes, I can't express how much I love them so this page will have to do. They are so cute, I want to hug them, squeeze them, and keep them all to myself. Their moms get a little worried when I squeeze them too hard. It's just my way of expressing how much I love them...

Nate

His favorite toys are Thomas the Tank Engine and Friends. His favorite website is Youtube. Trying to wean him from Youtube has been hard. Sometimes I videocall him and he shows me all his cool new toys and sings for me. I have a feeling there's something very fabulous about him. He's turning three this July but he'll always be a baby monkey in my eyes.
